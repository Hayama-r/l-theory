DESCRIPTION

Kasagi-dera Temple is a temple of the Chizan School of Shingon Buddhism located on Mt. Kasagi.

The entire Mt. Kasagi flourished as a training site for followers of the Shugendo religious belief.

The "Ryu-ketsu" cave is said to lead to "Tosotsuten", the abode of Maitreya Bodhisattva. A bodhisattva is a person who has not yet attained the full enlightenment that is necessary to become a Buddha but is on their way to do so.

It is famous for its autumn leaves, but you can also see beautiful green leaves in summer!
