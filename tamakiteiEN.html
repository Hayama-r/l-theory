DESCRIPTION

This delicious bakery is a five-minute walk from Ōbaku Station You can enjoy both Japanese breads such as rolls filled with curry and melon bread as well as European breads and pastries such as croissants and baguettes.

Many of the breads in the store are on display, so you do not need to worry about how to tell the staff what you want. Just show them. Also, even though it was a weekday, there were so many people that it was impossible to take many pictures inside the shop. You might want to head there as early as possible, given its popularity with the locals.

This is croissant dough filled with custard cream. The custard is not too sweet and you can enjoy the deliciousness of the fluffy croissant dough.

This pastry is called “Kougnané”. You cannot get this anywhere else except at this store! （320 Yen.） The custard is very tasty and not too sweet, but more importantly, the dough is crispy and very tasty. It is so delicious that it is difficult to describe in words how good it tastes, so please try it yourself.
